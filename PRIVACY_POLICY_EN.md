## 4. In-App Purchases and Transaction Data

The Service offers In-App Purchase (IAP) functionality, enabling users to purchase virtual goods through the Google Play Store.

### 4.1 Available Products

The Service offers the following paid products:

1. **Pigeon Virtual Currency**
   - Purpose: Purchase in-game items, upgrades, participate in limited-time events, and access game features.
   - Billing type: Consumable (can be purchased repeatedly).

2. **Exclusive Nickname Registration**
   - Purpose: Register an exclusive in-game nickname for display on leaderboards and social interactions.
   - Billing type: One-time purchase (lifetime ownership tied to the account).

### 4.2 Transaction Data Processing

In-app purchase transactions are processed by **Google Play Store**. The Service does not directly handle or store your financial information (such as credit card numbers).

For Google Play's privacy policy and payment terms, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

We may record the following transaction-related information:
- Transaction ID
- Purchase time
- Product type and quantity
- Purchase status (success/failure)

This data is used solely to:
- Verify transaction legitimacy
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Provide customer support and refund processing

### 4.3 Refunds and Cancellations

For in-app purchase refund requests, please contact **Google Play Store** directly.
We do not directly process refund requests but will adjust accounts accordingly based on Google Play notifications.

For Google Play's refund policy, please visit:
https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637
